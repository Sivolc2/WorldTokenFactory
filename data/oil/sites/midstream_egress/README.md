# Site: Midstream Egress — Waha Hub
**Coordinates**: 30.92°N, 102.47°W — Pecos County, Texas

## What's here
The Waha natural gas hub — a loose cluster of compressor stations and processing plants
where Permian Basin associated gas exits the basin. Waha is where the pipeline bottleneck
manifests: prices went negative on 38 of 51 days in 2026, and the Blackcomb pipeline
relief valve has been delayed to November 2026.

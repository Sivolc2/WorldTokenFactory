# Offshore Platform Hurricane Exposure Assessment
## Gulf of Mexico Operations

### Storm Track Analysis 2010–2023
Analysis of 18 named storms affecting operations area:
- Direct hits (category 2+): 4 events
- Near-miss (category 1, within 50nm): 7 events
- Total production downtime attributed to storms: 847 platform-days

### Platform Vulnerability Assessment
Current fleet: 12 fixed platforms, 3 FPSOs, 2 semi-submersibles

High-risk assets (category 3+ design envelope exceeded by 2050 projections):
- Platform GC-204: original design 1987, cathodic protection overdue
- Platform MC-311: mooring system last re-certified 2018
- FPSO Magnolia Star: designed for 100-year return period winds, regional estimates now project 85-year recurrence

### Insurance and Deductible Structure
- Operational downtime deductible: $15M per event
- Physical damage deductible: $25M per event
- Named storm exclusion active June 1 – November 30
- Three claims in past 5 years: $34M, $12M, $78M

### Regulatory Requirements
- BSEE 30 CFR Part 250 evacuation procedures: compliant
- Emergency disconnect testing: last completed Q1 2022 — Q1 2023 test delayed
